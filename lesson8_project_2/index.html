Who is Nyle Dimarco?

Nyle Dimarco is the first deaf person to ever win America Next Top Model and Dancing with the Stars. With his rise to fame, he became a major advocate for the Deaf comminuty. He currently is still modeling, spreads deaf awareness, and fights for equal rights of minorities.

Is deafness a disability? NO!

The oly difference there is between a hearing person and deaf person is that a deaf person can't hear. other than that, deaf people can do just about anything a hearing person can do.
